# House of Dance Strofes - Design Philosophy

## Design Movement
**Modern Elegance with Dynamic Energy** - A contemporary design that balances sophistication with the vibrant, energetic nature of dance.

## Core Principles
1. **Vibrant Energy**: Warm, saturated colors (golds, purples, deep oranges) that reflect the passion and movement of dance
2. **Clean Minimalism**: Ample whitespace and simple layouts that let content breathe and images shine
3. **Motion & Flow**: Smooth transitions and animations that echo the fluidity of dance
4. **Community Focus**: Warm, welcoming visual language that emphasizes inclusivity and joy

## Color Philosophy
- **Primary**: Deep purple (#6B4C9A) - elegance, creativity, passion
- **Accent**: Warm gold (#D4A574) - warmth, energy, celebration
- **Background**: Clean white (#FFFFFF) - clarity, simplicity, sophistication
- **Text**: Dark charcoal (#1A1A1A) - readability, professionalism
- **Gradient accents**: Purple to gold transitions for dynamic sections

## Layout Paradigm
- Hero section with full-width image and overlay
- Asymmetric card layouts for class offerings
- Centered content with generous padding
- Google Maps as a prominent, integrated feature
- Sticky navigation for easy access

## Signature Elements
1. **Gradient Dividers**: Smooth purple-to-gold transitions between sections
2. **Animated Spiral Background**: Subtle spiral animation in hero/background areas
3. **Warm Lighting**: Golden/warm tones in all imagery and overlays

## Interaction Philosophy
- Smooth hover effects on cards and buttons
- Subtle scale transforms on interactive elements
- Fade-in animations as content enters viewport
- Responsive feedback on all clickable elements

## Animation Guidelines
- Smooth easing functions (ease-in-out)
- Duration: 300-500ms for most interactions
- Subtle transforms: scale(1.05), translateY(-5px)
- Fade transitions for content changes
- Spiral animation in background for visual interest

## Typography System
- **Display Font**: Playfair Display (elegant, sophisticated headlines)
- **Body Font**: Inter (clean, modern, highly readable)
- **Hierarchy**: Bold display for headings, regular weight for body
- **Spacing**: Generous line-height (1.6-1.8) for readability
